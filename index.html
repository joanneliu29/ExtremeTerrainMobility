Welcome to Georgia Tech's team for NASA 2022 Big Idea Challenge

Goal:

Design, develop, and demonstrate robotic systems with alternative rover locamotion modalities for use in off-world extreme lunar terrain applications.

The built prototypes must incorporate at least minimal level of sensing, autonomy, etc. need for a relevant test.

Enhance or replace traditional wheeled mobility system that can expand our access to extreme terrain on the Moon and (later) on Mars.
